About Nohar Ben Asher

Nohar Ben Asher is an Israeli independent curator and lecturer. She holds an MFA in curating from Goldsmith University of London and a BA in Art History from Tel Aviv University. After graduating with honors the exchange programme at the Venice International University, Italy Ben Asher was a contributor for the Icelandic pavilion exhibition at the Venice Biennale of 2009. Upon return to Israel she has been the curator of Artplus Hotel art space, a frequent writer for Time Out Magazine, curator of Palestinian-Israeli art projects in collaboration with Amnesty International -Israel and PR campaigner for Tel Aviv City Hall Art Centre.
